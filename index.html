About Me

I'm currently studying for a Diploma in Immersive Media at Ngee Ann Polytechnic. Graduated from ITE College West in 2021 where I took Higher Nitec in IT Application Development. I was a CCA member of the OWN-IT club and was a Vice President of Apple Club from 2019-2020.


A few personal details about me, I am always looking and trying new things, mostly trying to find what are my strength and weakness. I am interested in cryptocurrency, video editing, and dream of exploring the world. My favourite hobbies is swimming and I am an avid computer gamer.


My true passion is computer building and repairs, I would be able to stay up all night trying to complete it for a friend or family. Any of my friends would call me up for inquiries if they need me to help. It all started in 2016 when I first received my first desktop computer after doing well in school as a reward. It was a second-hand computer and it was very slow. I wanted to buy a new computer because it was giving me a lot of problems. But when I found out that I could just upgrade my computer, it piques my curiosity and began taking apart my computer to see what I could improve it. I've gained insight into how things worked and how they were constructed. And everything just took off from there.
